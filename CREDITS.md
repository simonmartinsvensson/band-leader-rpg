# Credits

## Art

### Characters — LimeZu, "Modern Interiors" (free version)

The overworld player and NPC sprites are from LimeZu's **Modern Interiors** asset pack
(free version).

- Author: **LimeZu**
- Pack: **Modern Interiors (free version)**
- Link: <https://limezu.itch.io/moderninteriors>

The free-version sprites are repacked into the game's character format by
`scripts/repack-characters.py` (the raw pack is not redistributed in this repo — only the
repacked sprites the game loads are committed).

**License — non-commercial only.** Per the pack's `LICENSE.txt`, the free version may be used and
edited **in non-commercial projects only**; it may not be used in commercial projects, and the
sprites may not be edited and resold. **Band Leader RPG must remain non-commercial** while it ships
this art. If the project ever goes commercial, replace these sprites (e.g. with the paid Modern
Interiors version, which is licensed for commercial use) or with other appropriately-licensed art.

## Other assets

- **Tiles, battle musician placeholders (`player`/`npc`), bitmap font, music, and SFX** are
  placeholder art/audio generated by the scripts in `scripts/` (`gen-placeholders.mjs`,
  `gen-font.mjs`, `gen-audio.mjs`). The font is original CC0 art authored for this project.
